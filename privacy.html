Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Quizient is a fully client-side tool for converting spreadsheet-based question banks to Moodle® XML. Because all processing happens entirely within your browser, we collect and retain almost no personal information. This policy explains what happens to your data.

2. What Data Is Collected?

  • Uploaded files: The spreadsheets you select are parsed locally. They never leave your device and are not transmitted to any server.
  • Theme preference: If you switch between Light, Dark, or Auto, the choice is stored in localstorage under the key "quizient-theme" so the preference persists between visits.
  • Contact form submissions: If you send a message via the contact form, your name, email address, and message body are sent directly to Formspree for delivery to our inbox. Quizient does not see or store this data locally. See Formspree's Privacy Policy for more information.

No analytics, cookies, or other tracking technologies are used.

3. How Is Your Data Processed?

After you select a file, Quizient parses each row with SheetJS and generates XML with fast-xml-parser The resulting XML string is offered for download and is not sent anywhere else.

4. Storage & Retention

  • Your uploaded files remain in memory only for the duration of the browser session.
  • The generated XML is held in memory until you reload/close the page.
  • The only persistent item is the theme preference stored in localstorage; you can clear it any time via your browser settings

5. Hosting & Server Logs (GitHub Pages)

Quizient is hosted on GitHub Pages, a site hosting service provided by GitHub Inc. GitHub Pages may record standard web-server logs (e.g., IP address, date/time of request, user-agent) for operational reasons. These logs are managed by GitHub and are not accessible to Quizient.

6. Third-Party Libraries

Quizient is bundled with and relies on several open-source third-party libraries (SheetJS, fast-xml-parser, DOMPurify, tailwindcss, Lucide). They run only in your browser and do not transmit data externally. License details can be found on the legal notices page.

When you load the site, your browser also fetches the Exo 2 font from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com / fonts.gstatic.com), which reveals your IP address to Google. No other external requests are made.

7. Security

  • All traffic is served over HTTPS by GitHub Pages.
  • The site sets a strict Content-Security-Policy to mitigate XSS and related attacks.
  • File parsing occurs within your browser; nothing is executed on a backend.

8. Audience

Quizient is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone-child or adult.

9. Policy Changes

This page may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of the page reflects the most recent change.

10. Contact
